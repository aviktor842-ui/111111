PPC Campaign Plan

Paid search to drive buyers to a focused headphone buying-guide landing page.

PPC platform

Google Ads (Search Network) targeting people actively comparing noise cancelling headphones.

Campaign goal

Drive high-intent visitors to the Services landing page and encourage a click to the Contact CTA for personalized help.

Target audience

  • Commuters who want quieter trains and buses
  • Students who study in shared or noisy spaces
  • Remote and hybrid workers who need clearer calls
  • Frequent flyers looking for comfort and long battery life

Keywords

  • noise cancelling headphones (primary)
  • wireless noise cancelling headphones
  • best ANC headphones for commuting
  • active noise cancellation headphones
  • noise cancelling headphones microphone

Budget

Starter budget: $15/day for 30 days (~$450/month). Split into two ad groups: “best”/comparison intent and “commute”/use-case intent. Use location targeting if you ship to specific markets.

Ad example

Responsive Search Ad (example)

Headlines: Best Noise Cancelling Headphones • Quiet Commute Picks • Comfort & Battery Compared

Description: Compare top ANC options by comfort, battery life, and call quality. See which models suit travel, study, and work. View the checklist and picks now.

Final URL: services.html

Landing page reference: the ad points to Services, which links to Contact for a recommendation request.
